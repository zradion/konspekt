Few years ago he asked her out for a coffee and she had short conversations with him in the shop at work.    Nora emerged at the Midnight Library at 00:00:00 and time have stopped. It was solid, rectangular building about the size of a church. She could see pillars, stone facade, a large wooden central door, a grandeur roof, grand-looking clock on the front gable, tall dark arched windows. She went in. The place was well lit, and the floor was light stone. She had taken a few steps forward and could no longer see walls at all - just bookshelves. Aisles of shelves up to the ceiling and branching off from the broad corridor. She took one corridor and set off. She soon realized that she won't find a way out. The books were everywhere, all green with multifarious shades without titles or author names and the only other variation was size: similar height but varied in width. Despite the fact that the books looked old, the air in the library felt fresh. Here Nora saw the librarian, recognizing her as Mrs. Elm.

   Mrs.Elm told Nora that this was her library and every book here - is a version of her life. Than handed the book to Nora which wasn't like others. It was grey and heavy. That was Book of Regrets. Nora chose her main regret, then Mrs. Elm picked up a book with a new life for her. Nora began to read from the beginning and turned out in that life.
