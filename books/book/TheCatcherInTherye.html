The catcher in the rye

Published July 1951

characters and places:

   Holdon Caulfield - He is seventeen years old. He grew six and a half inches last year. He has gray hair on the right side since he was a kid. Used to be a heavy smoker. They made him cut it out. That's also a reason how he got t.b.
   D.B. - his brother; He used to be a regular writer, than he wrote that book - short stories - "The secret Goldfish". He is in Hollywood now, has Jaguar (very expensive english car) and a lot of dough.
   Pencey prep - school in Agerstown, Pennsylvania.
   Mr. and Mrs. Spencer - The couple both around seventy years old. He is the history teacher. They live on Anthony Wayne Avenue (not on the campus).

events:

   Holdon is writing his diary. He is at Pencey Prep, a rather prestigious school, but Holden doesn't see it that way. It's going to be Christmas vacation. He wasn't supposed to come back after on account of he was flunking four subjects and not applying himself and all.
   It is Saturday, December, very cold. He'd just got back from New York with the fencing team. The meeting did not take place due to the team's late arrival. Everyone blamed Holden for this and ostracized him the whole the way back.
   Holden is freezing his ass off on the Thomsen Hill, watching on the field - football between Saxon Hall and Pencey. The week before some crooks had stolen his camel's-hair coat and fur-lined gloves - The more expensive a school is, the more crooks it has. Realy, he's trying to feel some kind of a good-by.
   He visits the Spencers and he have to listen his notations. Everyone is trying to convince him to come to his senses.
