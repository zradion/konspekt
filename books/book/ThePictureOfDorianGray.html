The picture of Dorian Gray

Published in 1890; 82 pages.

characters and places:

Dorian Gray - Certainly wonderfully handsome, with his finely curved scarlet lips, his frank blue eyes, his crisp gold hair. Grandson of Lord Kelso. Mother of him - Lady Margaret.

Lady Margaret Devereaux - (mother of Dorian) was extraordinarily beautiful girl running away with a subaltern in a foot regiment. Lord Kelso got some rascally adventurer, some Belgian brute to insult and to kill that chap in a duel at Spa a few month after the marriage. He brought his daughter back with him. She never spoke to him again and died within a year.

Basil Hallward - the artist. He and Henry went to Oxford. Has got studio not far from London.

Lord Henry Wotton - Lives on Curson Street. Brown beard, olive-coloured face with worn expression and low languid voice, patent-leather boots, tasselled ebony cane. Smoking innumerable cigarettes.

Lady Henry Victoria - curious woman, wife of Henry. She dresses always as if it had been designed in a rage and put on in a tempest, usualy in love with somebody and her passion was never returned. And she has a perfect mania for going to church.

Lady Brandon - Where Basil have met Dorian.

Lady Agatha - aunt of Henry. We could see at her lunch: Duchess of Harley - very stoutness, admirable good-nature and good temper; Sir Thomas Burdon - a Radical member of Parlament; Mr. Erskine of Treadley - an old gentlman of considerable charm and culture, who was usualy silent; Mrs. Vandeleur - one of oldest friend of Lady Agatha, a perfect saint amongst women, but dradfully dowdy; Lord Faudel - a most intelligent middle-aged mediocrity;

Lord George Fermor - a genial if somewhat rough-mannered old bachelor. Uncle of Lord Henry. Has bushy white eyebrows. His father had been ambassador at Madrid, but had retired from the Diplomatic Service in a capricious. He considered that he was fully entitled by reason of his birth, his indolence, the good English of his dispatches and his inordinate passion for pleasure to have offer from Embassy at Paris. The son resigned along with him. And was doing paticulary nothing now. Only England could have produced him, and he always said that the country was going to the dogs.

Sibyl Vane - the actress. First love of Dorian.

events:

Studio of Basil Hallward not far from London. He almost finished the portrait of Dorian Gray. Here Basil introduces Lord Henry Wotton with Dorian. Dorian, when saw picture of his self said he would give his soul for he remain young and it grow old.

Next day Henry visited his uncle Lord Fermor to know about Dorian's parentage and diner at Lady Agatha where he offered how remain young - to commit the great error that one committed in early days.

First adventure of Dorian - Sibyl Vane. An actress of a little theater.
