The picture of Dorian Gray

Published in 1890; 82 pages.

characters and places:

Dorian Gray. Certainly wonderfully handsome, with his finely curved scarlet lips, his frank blue eyes, his crisp gold hair.

Basil Hallward - the artist. He and Henry went to Oxford. Have studio not far from London.

Lord Henry Wotton. Lives on Curson Street. Brown beard, patent-leather boots, tasselled ebony cane. Smoke innumerable cigarettes.

Lady Brandon. Where Basil have met Dorian.

Lady Agatha - aunt of Henry.

events:

Studio of Basil Hallward not far from London. He almost finished the portrait of Dorian Gray. Here Basil introduces Lord Henry Wotton with Dorian.
